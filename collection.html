Gallery 1A

Located on the first floor and is the largest exhibition floor. With the size of 1650 sq ft, this gallery is suitable to hold large exhibition for art painting, printing, sculpture, photography and installation. The area is equipped with controllable lighting system to create creative ambience. This gallery also houses exhibits and sculpture of famous artists.

Gallery 2A

Gallery 2A at the National Art Gallery in Malaysia is one of the exhibition spaces designed to showcase a variety of art forms. It is often used for temporary exhibitions, providing a platform for contemporary Malaysian artists and international artists to display their works. The gallery aims to foster public engagement and promote the appreciation of both traditional and modern art, often highlighting emerging trends in the art scene

Gallery 2B

Gallery 2B at the National Art Gallery in Malaysia is a dedicated space for contemporary art exhibitions. It hosts a range of temporary exhibitions, focusing on modern and experimental works by both local and international artists. The gallery often showcases thought-provoking and innovative art, highlighting emerging trends and cultural narratives.

Gallery 2C

The children’s gallery is a gallery exhibiting artworks by children. One of the activities are "Find the Similarities – Selected Portrait Photography" where The collection of 539 photography artworks is the collection of the National Visual Arts Development Board, presented as a gift by Dato’ Loke Wan Tho in 1963, during his tenure as a member of the first Board of Trustee of the National Visual Arts Development. A total of six portrait photography artworks were selected and exhibited at the Interactive Area of Gallery 2C.

Gallery 3A

Gallery 3A at the National Art Gallery in Malaysia is an exhibition space dedicated to displaying a mix of traditional and contemporary art. It is often used for showcasing permanent collections or thematic exhibitions that highlight the history and evolution of Malaysian art. The gallery offers visitors a deeper understanding of the nation's artistic heritage, featuring works by prominent Malaysian artists as well as significant pieces that reflect the country's cultural and historical context

Gallery 3B

Gallery 3B at the National Art Gallery in Malaysia is an exhibition space that typically features more specialized or focused exhibitions, often highlighting specific themes, movements, or artists. It serves as a platform for showcasing both local and international artworks, with an emphasis on diversity in artistic expression. The gallery may host rotating exhibitions that explore various aspects of visual art, offering visitors a unique opportunity to engage with different artistic ideas and perspectives.

Gallery REKA

Located on the 1st floor, this gallery provides facilities for display of photography, textiles, fashion, prototype, model, posters, architectural and instrument. The gallery has 36.52 meter wall, a 3.6 to 4.2 meter ceiling height and two mobile side panel measuring 2.44 meter x 1.22 meter. This gallery also feature Ultra Violet Filter.

Main Lobby

The main lobby is located at the first floor while the auditorium and the resource centre are located on the left. Resource centre provides information on arts for students and communities. It also has internet connection. Gallery 1 is at the right of the main lobby. It is suitable for big scale of display. There is also lifts and spiral stairs to go to floor 2 and 3.

Auditorium

This auditorium is equipped with 144 seats and venue suitable for film viewing, talk show and seminar. It can be rented to private companies and organization.

Resource Centre

Other than showcasing and assembling collection, National Art Gallery also provides Art Reference Resource Center facilities. The Centre features 4,500 books about arts of local and international publications. There are about 500 registered local artists’ bio data, collection of research papers, digital news reference and a corner for discussion. In addition, five (5) internet stations are provided at a minimal charge
