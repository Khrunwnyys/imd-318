Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main objective of National Art Gallery?

National Art Gallery's main objective is to hold performances within or from outside Malaysia, classes, seminars, competitions, and other arts and cultural activities. It also gives their best effort to enlarge the main collections which already consist of almost 2500 works.

What exhibitions are currently on display?

We feature both local and international artists. You can find details on the exhibitions page of our website or contact us for the current exhibition schedule.

How do I purchase tickets for events?

Tickets for events can be purchased directly through our website or on-site at the gallery. For more information, please visit our events page.

Do you offer guided tours?

Yes, we offer guided tours for both individuals and groups. You can book a tour via our contact page or by calling the gallery directly.

Can I hold an event at the National Art Gallery?

Yes, the gallery is available for private events. For inquiries about booking and availability, please visit our events booking page.
